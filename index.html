Amity International School,Noida

Hi!

Amity International School, Noida, is a school located in the National Capital Region of India. The school is located on a 15-acre (61,000 m2) complex in sector 44 (Noida), adjacent to Kalindi Kunj on the link road between Noida and Delhi. The school provides education from nursery to class XII. The campus is divided into six blocks to accommodate the pre-primary, primary, middle and senior wings. Block A: primary classes, junior library and pre-primary classes Block B: the transport office, office of the headmistress, primary classes, the amphitheatre and a multipurpose hall Block C: the office of the vice-principal and headmistress, senior classes, laboratories, the auditorium and senior library Block D: the administrative office, reception area, principal's office, and a multipurpose hall Block E: the office of the headmistress, middle classes, laboratories and activity rooms Block J: Senior School Synchro Study program classes, the Amity Five-Year Conceptual Program (AFYCP) classes and the hostel
